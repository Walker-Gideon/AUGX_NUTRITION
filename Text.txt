### Montserrat

Why it works:

* Clean and modern
* Strong for **headlines**
* Very popular with **startup brands**

Use it for:

* Headings
* Navigation
* Buttons

Example:

```
Fuel Your Body. Elevate Your Training.
```

Bold **Montserrat** looks powerful.

---

# 2️⃣ Strong Athletic Look

### Bebas Neue

This font screams **fitness brand**.

Best for:

* Hero headlines
* Product section titles

Example:

```
SERIOUS MASS
BUILD MUSCLE FASTER
```

But don't use it for paragraphs because it’s too condensed.

---

# 3️⃣ Clean Modern Body Text

### Inter

Inter is perfect for:

* paragraphs
* product descriptions
* small text

Very readable.

---

# 4️⃣ Perfect Font Combination for AUGX

Use **two fonts only**.

Recommended combo:

### Headings

**Bebas Neue**

### Body Text

**Inter**

Example structure:

```
HERO TITLE → Bebas Neue
Section Titles → Bebas Neue
Paragraphs → Inter
Buttons → Montserrat or Inter
```
